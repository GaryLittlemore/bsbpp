About The Competition

Welcome to the British Superbike Podium Predictor - a fun, season-long Facebook competition where BSB fans put their race knowledge to the test!


For each BSB round, participants predict the podium finishers (1st, 2nd & 3rd). One prediction covers all three races of the round, with points awarded for correct predictions and standings updated after every round to form an overall league table.


This competition is just for fun, bragging rights, and community engagement. All skill levels welcome!

How It Works

  • Predict the podium for each BSB round (1st, 2nd, 3rd)
  • Your prediction applies to all three races of that round
  • Earn points for correct predictions
  • Track your progress on the league table
  • Compete with other BSB fans throughout the season

Competition Rules

1. Eligibility

  • Open to anyone with a Facebook account.
  • Entry is free.
  • By participating, you agree to these rules.

2. How to Enter

  • For each BSB round, submit one podium prediction:
    • 1st place
    • 2nd place
    • 3rd place
  • This single prediction will apply to all three races of a round.
  • Predictions must be posted before the Free Practice 1 (FP1) begins for that round.
  • Late entries or edits after FP1 starts will not be counted.

3. Scoring System

Points are awarded per race, using the same prediction across all three races:

  • 5 points for a complete podium predicted correctly (all three riders in the correct positions).
  • 1 point for each individual podium position correctly predicted (correct rider in correct finishing position).
  • No points for correct riders in incorrect positions.

4. League Table

  • Points are totaled after each round.
  • A running league table will be posted on the page.
  • The league table will determine standings throughout the season.

5. Tie-Breakers

If required (e.g. end of season):

  1. Most correctly predicted full podiums
  2. Most total individual podium positions correct
  3. Earliest correct prediction submission for the final round

6. Changes & Cancellations

  • If a race is cancelled, no points will be awarded for that race.
  • If fewer than three races run in a round, scoring applies only to completed races.
  • The organiser reserves the right to clarify or amend rules if unforeseen situations arise.

7. Prizes

  • The competition is purely for fun.
